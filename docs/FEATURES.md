# Gimme — What's Built Today

A plain-language tour of every feature currently in the app. If you can tap it or see it, it's listed here.

> **Last updated:** May 2026

---

## The Basics

### Sign in your way
- **Email + password** account.
- **Forgot password** with reset email.
- **"Try it first"** — guest mode lets anyone use the app instantly, no signup. Their data is preserved if they create an account later.
- **Anonymous-to-real account upgrade** — guests can convert without losing history.
- **Session-expired banner** — if your login expires while you're playing, the app tells you exactly what's happening (no work lost).

### First-time experience
- **Guided onboarding** for first-run users — walks them through the basics.
- **Add to home screen** prompt — installs Gimme like an app on iPhone/Android without going through the app stores.

## Setting Up a Round

### Course handling
- **Pre-loaded course library** — hand-curated Ventura-area courses ready to go.
- **"Near me" course search** — auto-finds courses by GPS.
- **Shared course catalog** — courses that other Gimme users have built are available to everyone.
- **Custom course builder** — add any course manually with full hole-by-hole pars, stroke indexes, multiple tee boxes (Black, Blue, White, Red), yardages, ratings, and slopes.
- **9-hole or 18-hole** rounds, including front-9-only or back-9-only.
- **Shotgun start** for events (everyone starts at different holes simultaneously).

### Player handling
- **Personal player roster** — save players you golf with and reuse them.
- **Public player directory** — opt-in profiles other users can find.
- **Handicap-aware** — every game can be played gross or net.
- **Payment handles** — store Venmo, Zelle, Cash App, PayPal info on each player profile.
- **Up to 8 players** per round (was 4 originally).

## The 11 Side Games

Every game supports gross or net scoring. Stakes can be **money** ($5–$1,000+ buy-ins) or **points** (no money, just bragging rights).

| Game | What it is | Player count |
|---|---|---|
| **Skins** 🎰 | Win the hole, win the pot. Carryovers add to the next hole. Press option doubles the stakes. | Any |
| **Best Ball** 🤝 | Two-team match — best score on each hole counts for the team. | Any even number |
| **Nassau** 🏳️ | Three separate bets in one round: front 9, back 9, and overall 18. | Any |
| **Wolf** 🐺 | Rotating "wolf" each hole picks a partner — or goes solo for double points. | 3+ |
| **Bingo Bango Bongo (BBB)** ⭐ | First on green, closest to pin, first in hole — a point each. | 3+ |
| **Hammer** 🔨 | Two-player match-play with a doubling "hammer" you can throw. | 2 |
| **Vegas** 🎲 | Two-team format where each team's score combines two players' digits. | 4 |
| **Stableford** 📊 | Points per hole based on score relative to par (eagle = 4, birdie = 3, etc.). | Any |
| **Dots** 🔴 | Side bets for sandies, greenies, snakes, etc. — paid per occurrence. | Any |
| **Banker** 🏦 | One player matches up against everyone else hole-by-hole. | 3+ |
| **Quota** 📋 | Each player has a target Stableford score; performance vs. quota wins. | Any |

### Game add-ons
- **Junk / Dots side-bets** layered on top of any game (sandy, greenie, snake, polecat, custom).
- **Prop bets** — create "anyone make a birdie on hole 7?" wagers between players.
- **Carryovers** for skins (on/off toggle).
- **Stakes modes**: Standard ($5–$50 presets) and **High Roller** ($100–$1,000 presets, separate gold-themed UI).
- **Game presets** — save your favorite game configurations as one-tap templates.

## During the Round

### Score entry
- **Tap-and-go score input** — large number pad, optimized for one-handed phone use.
- **Score celebrations** — animated reactions for eagles, birdies, holes-in-one.
- **Hole-by-hole navigation** with stroke index, par, and yardage shown.
- **Live game status** — running totals, who's winning, current pot, presses available.
- **9-hole or 18-hole modes** with mid-round support.

### Multi-device sync
- **Real-time updates** — anyone in the round sees scores as they're entered.
- **Score approval workflow** — for events, group scorekeepers approve scores before they count.
- **Conflict detection** — if two devices edit the same score, the app catches it.
- **Background tab efficiency** — when the tab isn't visible, the app stops listening to save battery and data.

### Reliability
- **Offline mode** — keep entering scores when there's no signal; everything syncs when you reconnect.
- **Auto-save** with rapid-tap protection (correct a typo within a half-second and only the final value sticks).
- **Unsaved-changes warning** — back-button or tab close warns you if scores haven't synced yet.
- **Auto-recovery** — if anything errors out, the app tells you with a "back to home" button instead of crashing.

## Group Events (Tournaments / Outings)

For larger gatherings (12-32 people across 3-8 foursomes/fivesomes):

- **Event creation** with multi-group setup.
- **Auto-grouping** — assign players to foursomes automatically or manually.
- **Shotgun start tracking** — different starting holes per group, all scored on one event leaderboard.
- **Group scorekeepers** — designate one person per group as the official scorer for their group.
- **Score Master role** — event organizer can override and edit any score across any group.
- **Live event leaderboard** — overall standings + per-group views, presence indicators (who's online).
- **Spectator mode** — share a link (no signup) so non-players can follow scores live.
- **QR code invites** — generate a scan-to-join code players can use from the parking lot.

## Tournaments (Multi-round)

- **Stroke play** tournaments — multiple rounds, accumulated scoring.
- **Bracket play** tournaments — head-to-head match brackets with auto-generated pairings.
- **Tournament leaderboard** with running standings.
- **Tournament list** to track ongoing and past tournaments.

## After the Round — Settle Up

- **Automatic payout calculation** — combines every game type, every prop bet, every dot/junk bet into one settlement.
- **Net-out smart settlement** — minimizes the number of payments (instead of 6 people paying 6 different people, the app figures out the cleanest 2-3 payments).
- **Treasurer mode** — designate one person as the round treasurer; the app routes payments to them.
- **One-tap payment links** — direct deep links open Venmo, Zelle, Cash App, PayPal pre-filled with the right amount and memo.
- **Mark as paid** — track what's been settled vs. still owed.
- **Share results card** — generate a beautiful shareable image of round results for group chats.
- **Notification sent** to everyone who owes money.

## Personal Tools

- **Personal dashboard** — your stats at a glance: rounds played, scoring trends, course performance.
- **Round history** with full scorecards and filters.
- **Stats** — score distribution (eagles/birdies/pars/bogeys), most-played courses, head-to-head records.
- **Handicap tracking** — automatic handicap index calculation based on USGA-style differentials from your last 20 18-hole rounds.
- **Ledger** — running tally of who owes whom across all your rounds with a given person.

- **Per-game stakes overrides** — change buy-ins on the fly.
- **Buy-in tracking** — visual confirmation when each player has paid in.
- **High Roller mode** for $100+ rounds with a distinct UI.

## Behind the Scenes (the stuff users don't see but benefit from)

- **Live data sync** across all devices in a round.
- **Anti-cheat / anti-abuse** — invite-code rate limiting (10/min, 30/hr per user) to prevent guessing.
- **Per-group score isolation** — scorekeepers can only enter scores for their own group.
- **Optimistic locking** — when two phones edit the same hole, the app catches the conflict instead of silently overwriting.
- **Error tracking** (Sentry) — issues are reported to the developer automatically.
- **Sentence-level validation** — no negative buy-ins, no par-99 holes, no infinity scores.

## What's NOT in the app yet

(Just so you can see the gaps clearly.)

- No Apple Watch companion.
- No Apple Pay / Google Pay direct (uses Venmo/Zelle/Cash App/PayPal).
- No GPS yardage on each shot during play.
- No course photos or hole flyovers.
- No social feed / round comments.
- No subscription / paid tier yet (everything is free).
- Not yet in the Apple App Store or Google Play Store (it's a web app you install from your browser).
