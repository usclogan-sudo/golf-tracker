# Couples Round Workflow

**Scenario:** You and your wife are at the course, both have the app on your phones, and you want to play a round together with a side game.

---

## Player A (You) — Creates the Round

### Step 1: Start New Round
- Open the app and tap **Start New Round**
- (Or **High Roller Round** if you're feeling spicy)

### Step 2: Pick a Course
- Search or scroll to find your course
- Tap it to select
- If the course isn't listed, tap **+ Add New Course** to create it

### Step 3: Select Players
- You're auto-included
- Search for your wife's name and tap to add her
- If she's not in your player list yet, use the **Quick Add** field to add her by name
- **2 players selected** — tap **Next**

> Groups step is skipped automatically for 5 or fewer players.

### Step 4: Choose Game & Stakes
- Pick a game type (e.g., **Skins**, **Nassau**, **Best Ball**)
- Tap the **?** on any game to read the rules
- Set the buy-in amount (preset or custom)
- Configure game-specific options (gross/net, carryovers, etc.)

### Step 5: Collect Buy-ins & Assign Roles
- **Treasurer** — select who holds the pot (probably you or her)
- **Game Master** — select who enters scores (this person controls the scorecard)
- Mark buy-ins as collected (or check "Start anyway" to skip)
- Tap **Create Round**

### Step 6: Share the Invite
- You'll see a **6-character invite code** and a **shareable link**
- Text the link to your wife, or just read her the code
- Tap **Start Scoring** to open your scorecard

---

## Player B (Your Wife) — Joins the Round

### Step 1: Open the App
- Tap the link you texted her — the app opens and auto-fills the code
- OR tap **Join a Round** on the home screen and type the 6-character code

### Step 2: Find the Round
- The app shows the round details: course name, game type, players
- She confirms it's the right round

### Step 3: Claim Her Player
- She sees the player list and taps **her name**
- She's now linked to that player — tap **Start Scoring** to open her scorecard

---

## Playing the Round

### Who Enters Scores?
The **Game Master** (chosen in Step 5) enters scores for both players each hole. The other player can see the scorecard update in real time but cannot edit scores.

**Typical flow each hole:**
1. Game Master opens the scorecard (it shows the current hole, par, stroke index)
2. Both of you putt out
3. Game Master taps the score stepper for each player to record strokes
4. Swipe or tap to advance to the next hole
5. The other player's phone updates automatically

### What the Non-Game-Master Sees
- Live scorecard with both players' scores
- Running game status (skins pot, carry amounts, match state, etc.)
- Leaderboard tab showing standings
- They **cannot** edit scores — only view

### If You Lose Connection
- Scores entered offline are queued locally
- They auto-sync when you're back online
- A yellow "Offline" banner shows at the top of the scorecard

---

## After the Round

### Ending the Round
- The Game Master taps the **menu (three dots)** in the header
- Tap **End Round**
- Confirm — the app navigates to **Settle Up**

### Settle Up
- Shows the final scoreboard, game results, and who owes whom
- The **Treasurer** can mark payments as collected
- Both players get a summary: "You collect $X" or "You owe $X"
- Payment links (Venmo, Zelle, etc.) appear if profiles have them set up
- Tap **Done** to go home

---

## Tips for Couples Play

| Tip | Why |
|-----|-----|
| **Both create accounts** | Lets both players see live scores and get notifications |
| **Set up payment methods in Settings** | Venmo/Zelle links show up on the Settle Up screen |
| **Pick Skins or Nassau for 2 players** | Best Ball needs even teams; Wolf needs 3+; Hammer is built for exactly 2 |
| **Make one person Game Master** | Avoids confusion about who enters scores |
| **Use the invite link, not the code** | One tap to join vs. typing 6 characters |

## Quick Reference: Game Compatibility (2 Players)

| Game | Works with 2? | Notes |
|------|:---:|-------|
| Skins | Yes | Each hole is a head-to-head skin |
| Nassau | Yes | Front 9 / Back 9 / Overall — classic |
| Hammer | Yes | Built for exactly 2 players — double-or-nothing each hole |
| Stableford | Yes | Points-based, compare totals at end |
| Dots | Yes | Side achievements (sandies, greenies, etc.) |
| Quota | Yes | Each gets a target based on handicap |
| Best Ball | No | Needs even number of teams (4+ players) |
| Wolf | No | Needs 3+ players |
| BBB | Yes | Bingo Bango Bongo — works but more fun with groups |
| Vegas | Yes | Team game, but works as 1v1 variant |
| Banker | Yes | Rotating banker each hole |
