## Step 1: Open & Install (2 min)

1. Open the app URL on your phone's browser
2. You should see a login screen
3. Tap **"Try it first"** to start as a guest (no signup needed)
4. If you see an "Add to Home Screen" banner, go ahead and install it

**Tell me:**
- [ ] Did the page load quickly?
- [ ] Was the "Try it first" button obvious?
- [ ] If you installed it, did the app icon and splash screen look right?
- [ ] Anything confusing on the first screen?

---

## Step 2: Find or Add a Course (2 min)

1. From the home screen, tap **"New Round"**
2. You'll be asked to pick a course. Tap **"Add New Course"**
3. Try **"Find courses near me"** — allow location access
4. Pick a course from the list (or add one manually with a name + 9 or 18 holes)

**Tell me:**
- [ ] Did "Near Me" find courses close to you?
- [ ] Did the course cards with photos look good?
- [ ] If you added a course manually, was it clear what to enter?
- [ ] Anything frustrating?

---

## Step 3: Set Up a Round (3 min)

1. After picking a course, you'll set up the round
2. Add **2 players** (can be fake names — e.g., "Player A" and "Player B")
3. Pick a game — try **Skins** (simplest)
4. Set a stake amount (e.g., $1 per hole)
5. Start the round

**Tell me:**
- [ ] Was it clear how to add players?
- [ ] Did picking a game type make sense? Did you understand what "Skins" means from the description?
- [ ] How did the overall setup flow feel — too many steps? Too few?
- [ ] Anything you'd change?

---

## Step 4: Score a Few Holes (3 min)

1. You should now be on the **Scorecard** screen
2. Enter scores for **holes 1 through 3** for both players (just make up numbers — pars and bogeys)
3. Tap a player's score box, enter the number, move to the next

**Tell me:**
- [ ] Was the scoring interface intuitive?
- [ ] Could you easily tell whose score you were entering?
- [ ] Did the number pad work well on your phone?
- [ ] Could you see running totals / who's winning?
- [ ] Anything awkward or slow?

---

## Step 5: Check the Leaderboard & Settle Up (2 min)

1. After entering a few holes, tap **"Finish Round"** (or navigate to settle up)
2. Look at the results — who won? Who owes what?
3. Check if payment method links show up (Venmo, etc.)

**Tell me:**
- [ ] Were the results clear — could you immediately tell who won and who owes what?
- [ ] Did the payout math look right for a skins game?
- [ ] Was anything confusing about the settlement screen?

---

## Step 6: Explore (3 min)

Poke around anything else that catches your eye:
- **Round History** — see past rounds
- **Settings** — change your name, set a handicap, add a payment method
- **Home screen** — does it give you a good overview?

**Tell me:**
- [ ] What feature surprised you (in a good way)?
- [ ] What did you expect to find but couldn't?
- [ ] Would you use this app for your actual golf group? Why or why not?
- [ ] What's the #1 thing you'd change?

---

1. Go to **Settings**

**Tell me:**

- [ ] Did signup work smoothly?
