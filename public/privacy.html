Gimme — Privacy Policy

Side Games · Scores · Settled

Last updated: July 2026

Gimme ("Gimme," the "App," "we," "us," or "our") is operated by PELORUS HX LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use Gimme (at gimme.gg and in our iOS and Android apps), how we use it, and the choices you have.

Gimme is a scorekeeping and "settle-up" tracker for golf side games. We do not process, hold, transfer, or move any money. Payment usernames you choose to add are shown to your own playing group so they can settle up directly using their own third-party payment apps.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

  • Account information: your email address (for registered accounts) and password (stored securely, hashed, by our authentication provider). You may also use the App as a guest without an account.
  • Profile information: display name, optional golf handicap index, and an optional avatar image.
  • Payment usernames (optional): Venmo, Zelle, Cash App, and/or PayPal identifiers you add so your group can pay each other. These are handles/usernames only — we never collect card numbers or bank credentials, and we never move funds.
  • Golf activity: rounds, hole-by-hole scores, courses and players you create, game/side-game selections, and settle-up (who-owes-whom) records.
  • Communications: feedback or support messages you send us.

Information collected automatically

  • Device & push tokens: if you enable notifications on a mobile device, we store a device push token to deliver them.
  • Basic technical data: app version and limited diagnostic/crash information to keep the App working.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide the App: create rounds, keep score, calculate side games, and show settle-up totals.
  • To sync your data across your devices and let invited players join your rounds and events.
  • To display your chosen payment usernames to members of your group so they can settle up directly.
  • To send notifications you've opted into (e.g., round invites, score approvals, settle-up reminders).
  • To respond to support requests and improve the App.

3. How your information is shared

  • With your playing group: your display name, scores, and any payment usernames you add are visible to the other players in rounds and events you participate in.
  • Service providers: we use Supabase (database, authentication, and storage) and cloud hosting/notification providers to run the App. They process data on our behalf under their own security commitments.
  • We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for third-party advertising.
  • Legal: we may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and users.

4. Payments and third-party apps

Gimme does not process payments. When you tap a payment link, you are handed off to a third-party app or website (such as Venmo, PayPal, Zelle, or Cash App) that you control. Any transaction happens entirely within that third party's service and is governed by their terms and privacy policies, not ours.

5. Data retention and deletion

We keep your information while your account is active. You can delete your data and your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account in the App, which removes your profile, rounds, scores, and related data and closes your account. You may also request deletion by emailing us at the address below.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures — encryption in transit, hashed passwords, and access controls (row-level security) — to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data.

7. Children's privacy

Gimme is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including California residents under the CCPA/CPRA), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and to not be discriminated against for exercising them. You can exercise these rights in-app or by contacting us. We do not sell personal information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted here with an updated "Last updated" date.
